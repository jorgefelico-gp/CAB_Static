This will vary based on the studies or conversations we have coming up. We will try our best to contact you only when we think you will be interested, but please note that participation is your choice. The emails will come from a globalpay.com email address.

We invite you to share your feedback in many different ways. Most times, it takes place remotely via video conferencing or an online research tool. You may be asked to have a conversation with a Product Manager, Designer, or Researcher to understand how you work or for feedback on a new feature concept. Or you may be asked to fill out an online survey. Our goal is to understand your needs and how you experience our products. The time may vary from 10 minutes to 1 hour, but we will always inform you of the commitment when contacting you.

By participating, your feedback will go directly to our product and design teams. For some interactions, after participation, we may include a thank you gift like a gift card or swag, but this varies session by session. Details will be provided in study invitation emails.

The data collected in the opt-in form is to recruit and contact participants for future Global Payments studies and conversations in accordance with our Privacy Statement.
